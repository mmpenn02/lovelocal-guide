A LOVE LETTER TO YOU

Welcome to the CCPE "Love Local" Guide to the Conway and Central AR area.

This guide has been curated by the admins as a way for like-minded individuals in our area to mingle and support businesses we love. Conway (and the surrounding area) is a thriving community, teeming with diversity, local artists, and rich culture. You just have to know where to look!

This intro is a love letter to you: thank you for taking interest in our group and recommendations. Our community is only as big as you are.

— with love, Melody and Sydney, (CCPE Admins)

Food, Beverage & Grocery

🍔 Casual Eats
```
The Rogue Roundabout
💙
Local fave bar + eatery downtown. Known for good vibes and a diverse crowd. Offers outdoor, vintage clothing pop-ups every few months and IPA tastings. Great spot to land on weekends.
Big Bad Breakfast
⭐ 🍀
Southern breakfast done right. Expect a wait on weekends, but so worth it. Excellent for brunch dates. Allergy friendly, closes early! (It is breakfast, after all!)
Bulgogi
⭐ 💙 🍀
Korean BBQ in Conway, and genuinely good. Rare find in central AR. Bring friends to share the grill. Sweet owners, nut-allergy accommodations and vegetarian options.
Sakura Ocean
🍀 ⭐
Japanese and sushi. A solid go-to when you're craving sushi, and they offer an excellent ALL YOU CAN EAT OPTION! Accommodates gluten-free, soy-free, and for peanut allergies.
Brick & Forge Tap Room
Craft beer bar with rotating taps and a chill atmosphere. Beer on tap here is our fav. Great for low-key hangouts and game nights, also plays other televised events like the Super Bowl, The Grammys, and more! Lots of cheap eats here, you must try the pulled pork nachos!
Portofino Italian
Cozy Italian with a slightly more elegant atmosphere. Perfect for date night without going to Little Rock.
Taziki's Cafe
Mediterranean with solid vegetarian and lighter options. Great for lunch.
David's Burgers
✳️
Arkansas legend. Smash burgers, crinkle fries, milkshakes. Free ice cream samples when you dine in.
O'Malley's
🎵
Our own little Irish pub. Good bar food, drinks, and local music nights. Friendly neighborhood feel.
Rock N Roll Sushi
Funky sushi rolls with fun names and a party playlist.
Stoby's
✳️ 🐾
A Conway Classic. The cheese dip is mandatory. Casual diner vibe that locals swear by.
La Pista
Wood-fired pizza and Italian food with a vibey atmosphere, good desserts, worth the hype.
Roost & Tap
A New Favorite to the area! Southern comfort food with a tap list. Popular with the after-work crowd and live music fans.
Taj Mahal
Indian food in Conway, a genuine gem. Rich curries, friendly staff.
The Patio Cafe
🐾
Outdoor seating, casual brunch/lunch menu. Dog-friendly patio is a big plus for people with pets!
Hideaway Pizza Co.
Artisan pizza, a local chain with a "summer camp" atmosphere. Good for big groups.
Serrano's
Tex-Mex staple. Margaritas hit different here, ask anyone. Good for a casual night out.
Purple Cow
✳️
Local diner chain with nostalgia energy. Great milkshakes. A uniquely AR experience.
U.S. Pizza Company
A Conway classic. Reliable, casual, good for groups.
Whole Hog BBQ
✳️
Arkansas BBQ done right. Pulled pork, ribs, and a killer white potato salad. Won Best BBQ of Conway in 2023 for a reason!
Lost Pizza
Casual pizza spot, reliable and semi-local chain.
Los 3 Portillos
Mexican restaurant with solid home-style cooking.

Things To Do

🎨 Arts & Culture
```
Conway Art Walk
💙
Monthly downtown arts festival. 60+ artists, musicians, food vendors, and gallery openings. The best night to be in Conway. Check createconway.com.
Conway Art Center
Local pottery studio and community art hub. Frozen yogurt served here!
Baum Gallery at UCA
⭐
University art gallery with student and professional exhibits. Free, on UCA campus.
Windgate Museum of Art at Hendrix
⭐
Traveling exhibits, beautiful facility. Regularly free and open to the public.
Arkansas Shakespeare Theatre
⭐
AR's only professional Shakespeare company. Summer festival is a must. Featured in the New York Times.
Reynolds Performance Hall at UCA
⭐
Hosts big-name concerts, Broadway shows, and more. 1,200-seat venue on UCA campus. Check uca.edu/pa for tickets.
Red Curtain Theater
Community theater with a progressive reputation.
Full Moon Records
🎵 💙
Independent record shop. Find vinyl, support local music culture. LOCAL LIVE MUSIC VENUE!
King's Live Music
🎵
Local venue for live music. Often features indie, folk, and more.
